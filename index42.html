My name is Obed Nyangena, a passionate economist, data and policy advisor dedicated to making economics accessible and impactful for everyone. Currently I serve as Principal Planning Officer at the National Mining Corporation, and I am an active member of both the Economists Society of Kenya (ESK) and the International Society for Development and Sustainability.

My journey into economics began at the University of Nairobi, Kenya, way back in 2010, where I discovered my fascination with data, policy and the power of research to shape real-world outcomes amid uncertainty. Since then, I have worked across government, academia and consultancy, helping organizations and individuals mold and shape data-driven insights to detect and harness opportunities, deter and solve complex challenges in economic development, climate change, security, health and public policy.

I created this platform with a strong conviction that lessons learnt over the years can have a forceful multiplier effect and help have a better world to live in- whether it is generating and simplifying economic data, guiding research or offering practical policy advice - so that others can build their own expertise and drive positive change in their organizations and communities.

Reach out for incisive and metricized ideas in economics, strategy or policy research. Be rest assured that I will guide you to not only understand how data can make a difference but also customize products that can drive the growth you so much desire.
