Projects (Selected Articles)

Throughout the years, I have continued to make insightful contributions through research, scholarship, and innovation for purposes of advancing knowledge, shaping public policy debates and discourses. My Open Researcher and Contributor IDentifier (ORCID) is 0000-0003-4205-6136. While ORCID centralizes these works by way of attribution and tracking researcher contributions and affiliations across time, disciplines, and borders, I labored to provide the same here deliberately. Some of these works include:

Digital Fragility, Assorted Risks and Terrorism Confluence

With Mavia Imbali

Explores data privacy and global espionage. Advocates for updated legislation to combat malicious data acquisition.

SARS-CoV-2 Vaccine Uptake in Kenya

With Bertha A. Ocholla et al.

Analyzes disparities in vaccine uptake using demographic and occupational data. Offers insights for health policy.

Climate Change-Urbanization Nexus

With Victor Senelwa, Benson Igesa

Links urbanization and economic growth to carbon emissions in East Africa. Challenges passive narratives.

Weather Variability & Economic Growth

Guided by George Ruigu, PhD

Uses semi-parametric modeling to link weather patterns with East Africa’s growth. Urges for sustainable policy.

Climate Change, Pollinators and Cereal Yields

With Rachel Ngesa, Victor Senelwa

Shows the vital role of pollinators in cereal yield. Offers future data modeling recommendations for agriculture.

Impact of Weather Variability on Kenya's Economy

Master’s dissertation supervised by George Ruigu, PhD

Employs a Vector Error Correction Model to evaluate climate impacts on GDP. Supports short-term policy action.

Drought and Floods: Gender & Devolution in Kenya

With Rachel Ngesa, Antony Okeyo

Published in *Gender, Politics and Governance in Africa*. Explores gender roles in climate adaptation frameworks.
