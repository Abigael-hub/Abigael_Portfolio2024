Digital Fragility, Assorted Risks and Terrorism Confluence

With Mavia Imbali

We explore a digitally connected world and the tension between increased data demand and growing concerns over data protection and privacy. This paper discusses digital data value chains and their implications for individuals and organizations amidst a wave of global espionage.

Association of Demographic and Occupational Factors with SARS-CoV-2 Vaccine Uptake in Kenya

With Bertha A. Ocholla, Herbert K. Murayi, Jane W. Mwangi, Steve K. Belle, Phanice Ondeko, Rebecca Kendagor

This research examines initial governmental measures to curb COVID-19 spread in Kenya using demographic and occupational lenses. It reveals disparities in vaccine uptake and offers metricized recommendations.
